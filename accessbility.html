Web Accessibility & ADA Statement

Polytechnic@UofM is committed to providing a web experience that is accessible to all users, including individuals with disabilities. Our goal is to design and maintain Polytechnic web pages in a manner that aligns with applicable accessibility standards and the University of Memphis accessibility policies.

Accessibility goals

We aim to:

  • Use clear headings, labels, and link text.
  • Provide sufficient color contrast and readable text sizing.
  • Support keyboard navigation and screen reader users wherever possible.
  • Continuously review Polytechnic pages for accessibility issues and improve them over time.

Reporting an accessibility issue

If you encounter a barrier while using a Polytechnic@UofM web page or need content in an alternate format, please let us know:

  • Email: polytechnic@memphis.edu (placeholder)
  • University Accessibility Resources: University of Memphis Accessibility Resources

Feedback

We welcome feedback from students, faculty, staff, and community members about how we can improve accessibility across Polytechnic digital resources.
