Research & Applied Projects

Polytechnic@UofM emphasizes applied, workforce-connected projects. This page can highlight active research, community partnerships, and student capstones that demonstrate the value of Polytechnic programs.

Workforce-Ready IT Systems

Information Technology, Networking, Security

Projects focused on preparing students to deploy, secure, and manage IT systems in logistics, healthcare, and public-sector environments.

Manufacturing Leadership & Quality

Manufacturing Supervision

Applied projects that explore lean operations, safety, and leadership practices in advanced manufacturing settings.

Construction Leadership & Project Delivery

Construction Leadership

Case studies and community-focused projects that help students understand scheduling, coordination, and safety in construction.

Digital Workforce & Analytics

Cross-program, Analytics

Projects that teach students to interpret data, build dashboards, and apply analytics to real operational questions.
