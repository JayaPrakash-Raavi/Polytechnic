Labs & Facilities

Polytechnic@UofM connects students with applied learning spaces that support simulations, hands-on practice, and technology-rich instruction. Use this page to feature the key facilities that make the Polytechnic experience unique.

Simulation & Immersive Learning Lab

A flexible lab for simulation-based learning, including virtual reality and advanced training tools that mirror real-world environments.

Example uses: safety training, logistics workflows, and human–technology interaction scenarios.

Technology-Enabled Classrooms

Classrooms equipped with modern instructional technology, allowing faculty to demonstrate systems, software, and applied workflows in real time.

Example uses: live demonstrations of enterprise systems, data dashboards, and project collaboration.

Computing & Analytics Lab

A dedicated space for students to work with data, analytics tools, and applied IT projects in a collaborative environment.

Example uses: networking labs, analytics exercises, and technology-focused group projects.

Manufacturing & Applied Systems Space

A lab focused on manufacturing supervision, operations, and systems used in production environments.

Example uses: process mapping, safety exercises, and case studies based on real manufacturing scenarios.

Collaboration & Project Areas

Informal project areas where student teams can work on Polytechnic-related assignments, capstones, and community-focused projects.
