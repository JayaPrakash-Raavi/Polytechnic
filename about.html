About Polytechnic@UofM

Polytechnic@UofM is a new academic unit within the Herff College of Engineering at the University of Memphis. Launching in Fall 2026, it focuses on practice-oriented, technology-rich education that prepares students for high-demand careers in high-need industries.

Polytechnic@UofM offers condensed, workforce-aligned curricula (typically 30–40 credits) with alternative math options and flexible electives. Students can pair technical programs with other majors and earn credit through transfer coursework, industry certifications, and documented real-world experience.

Mission

To develop career-ready graduates who can design, build, manage, and improve the technologies and systems that power modern organizations.

Vision

To serve as a regional hub for applied technology education, workforce development, and innovation that supports industry, government, and community partners.

Workforce-aligned design

Programs are created with input from employers in logistics, manufacturing, construction, and related sectors. This alignment helps ensure that Polytechnic graduates bring relevant, immediately applicable skills to the workplace.

Flexible pathways for diverse learners

Polytechnic@UofM is designed for first-time students, transfer students, and working professionals. Pathways recognize what learners already know and support efficient progress toward workforce-ready degrees and certificates.
