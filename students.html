Students

The Polytechnic@UofM is designed around students who want workforce-ready qualifications and flexible pathways. Condensed curricula, alternative math options, and credit for prior learning help more learners move into high-need careers.

Condensed Curricula

Polytechnic degree pathways are typically 30–40 credits, with focused coursework aligned with specific workforce needs.

Flexible Credit Options

Programs can recognize transfer credit, industry certifications, and documented real-world experience where appropriate.

Pair with Other Majors

Polytechnic pathways are designed so students can pair technical programs with other majors to build unique combinations of skills.
